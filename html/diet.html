Chicken and Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients: 300g of sweet potatoes, 200g of lean chicken breast, tomatoes, onion and garlic powder.

How to:
Wrap the sweet potatoes with a paper towel or a thin plastic bag (plain, transparent, with no logos on it to avoid transfering that to the food while cooking). With a knife or fork, make small holes in the potatoes. Wrapped, put them in the microwave on high for 5 to 6 minutes. While it is cooking, preheat a shallow pan with a little bit of olive oil. Separate your chicken breasts, cut unwanted fats with a knife. Beat the meat with the back of the knife while being careful not to rip it in two. When the oil is hot, place the chicken in there. Cook it throughout until tender. Place sliced tomatoes and onion in the same pan. Garlic powder or pepper to taste. By the time the chicken is cooked you will have soft and nutritious sweet potatoes waiting for you in the microwave. Enjoy!

Lean Pizza

Ingredients: 2 cups of couliflower, 2 whole eggs, 1/2 cup of shredded low fat cheese, 3/4 cup of pasta sauce, 6oz of chicken, cut red peppers and oregano.

How to:
Shred the couliflower using a blender, it will be the substitute of flour. Mix the couliflower in a bowl with the eggs, cheese and oregano. Grab a round pan and preheat the oven to 450 degrees. This step is to make the dough. Lay out the previously mixed ingredients into the pan like a pizza shape. Cook it for about 15-20 minutes. Barbeque the chicken and cut it into small pieces. Once the dough is gold and done, add the chicken, more cheese, pasta sauce, and peppers on top of the dough. Put it back in the oven for about 10 minutes. Enjoy!

Super Shake

Ingredients: 4 tbs of low fat peanut butter, low fat chocolate powder, 2 bananas, 2 tbs nutella, sliced apples, powdered coffee, whey protein and water.

How to:
Slice the banana into thin pieces to avoid clogging the blender. Add the nutella, peanut butter and apples. Add whey protein to your particular goals. Add water until your desired consistency. This is a highly anabolic shake that will wake you up in the morning, getting your calories, carbs and protein in. Also good as a pre-workout shake. Enjoy!

High Protein Mess

Ingredients: Red peppers, 6oz of lean breast chicken, garlic powder, sliced corn and 1-2 cups of cottage cheese.

How to:
Barbeque, grill or roast the chicken to your taste. Use olive oil for a more healthy option. Add the garlic, corn, peppers and corn to your chicken. Once all veggies and meat are evenly cooked, add the cottage cheese and mix everything up in a bowl. This is a super high protein option that is easy to make and will satisfy your muscles for hours. Enjoy!

High Protein Cookies

Ingredients: 4 white eggs, low fat chocolate powder, low fat peanut butter, artificial sugar and thin oats.

How to:
Mix all the ingredients in a pan previously sprayed with cooking spray. Mix it with your hands until you get a dough consistency. Preheat the oven at 450 degrees. Mount the dough in cookie shaped amount in the pan or use a cookie pan. Let it cook it in the oven until golden. Enjoy!

Super Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients: Lettuce, tomatoes, light mayo, mustard, many slices of turkey breast (low sodium suggested), low fat cheese and whole wheat roll bread.

How to:
Lightly toast your bread on a skillet. Add your lettuce first, followed by the cheese, turkey and tomatoes. Add mayo and mustard to taste. The turkey has a high amount of protein that will keep you filled for a few hours. This a a very practical meal, you can make three or four sandwiches like this and bring it to work or school to help cover those meals and get your calories in. Enjoy!
